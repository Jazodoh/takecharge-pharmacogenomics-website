TakeCharge Pharmacogenomics Lab Testing

Business idea and purpose: TakeCharge Pharmacogenomics Lab Testing is a customer-facing educational website that explains pharmacogenomics and how lab testing can help people understand how they may respond to certain medications. 
The goal is to make the topic easier to understand and help users have better conversations with a licensed healthcare provider.

Platform used: Lovable

Website structure: Home, About, Services, Contact (FAQ optional)

Live website URL: https://gene-guide-you.lovable.app/
